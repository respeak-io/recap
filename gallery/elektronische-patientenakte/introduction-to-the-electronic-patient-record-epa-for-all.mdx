## Overview and Vision

The introduction of the Digital Act in Germany mandates the establishment of an Electronic Patient Record (ePA) for all insured individuals, known as 'ePA for all'. [video:00:00] This initiative aims to align Germany's healthcare system with that of many European neighbors, fostering a truly connected healthcare ecosystem free of information gaps. [video:00:20] The ePA is envisioned as a foundational component to significantly improve future patient care.
