## Initial Public Opinion on the ePA

Initial public opinion on the Electronic Patient File (ePA) in Germany shows a mixed understanding. Some individuals express little concern about data privacy, stating, "Kann doch jeder wissen, was ich für Zipperleiden habe" (Everyone can know what minor ailments I have) [video:00:00]. Others are somewhat aware of the ePA but lack detailed knowledge [video:00:00].

## Defining the ePA

In Germany, the ePA (elektronische Patientenakte) is a digital patient file intended for the administration of medical data for approximately 75 million statutory insured individuals [video:00:00].
