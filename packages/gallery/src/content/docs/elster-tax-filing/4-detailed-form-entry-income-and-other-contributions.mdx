## Entering Income and Tax Deductions (Anlage N)

The `Anlage N` is critical for employees, as it includes your salary and various income-related expenses [video:05:07]:

1.  Navigate to 'Anlage N'.
2.  Select the relevant person (e.g., 'Gordon Gekko').
3.  Go to '1 - Angaben zum Arbeitslohn' (Information on Wages).
4.  Enter your gross annual salary (e.g., '45.000,00 €') [video:05:20].
5.  In 'Zeile 6', enter the `Lohnsteuer` (Wage Tax) amount from your `Lohnsteuerbescheinigung` (e.g., '6.210,00 €') [video:05:25].
6.  In 'Zeile 8', enter the `Kirchensteuer des Arbeitnehmers` (Employee Church Tax) amount from your `Lohnsteuerbescheinigung` (e.g., '558,84 €') [video:05:30].
7.  Click 'Eintrag übernehmen' (Accept entry).

## Commuting Expenses (Anlage N - Entfernungspauschale)

You can claim the `Entfernungspauschale` (commuting allowance) for your daily commute to work, regardless of the mode of transport (e.g., public transport, bicycle, on foot) [video:05:46]:

1.  Navigate to 'Anlage N'.
2.  Go to '8 - Entfernungspauschale' (Commuting Allowance).
3.  Click 'Daten hinzufügen' (Add data) to add a new commuting entry.
4.  Specify the 'Ziel des Weges' (Destination) as 'Erste Tätigkeitsstätte' (Primary Place of Work).
5.  Enter the full address of your workplace (e.g., 'Moritzstraße 1, 13597 Berlin') [video:05:50].
6.  **Period**: Enter the period you commuted (e.g., '01.01.-31.12.') [video:06:05].
7.  **Workdays per Week**: Enter the number of workdays per week (e.g., '5') [video:06:05].
8.  **Non-Commuting Days**: Enter the total number of days you were on vacation, sick, worked from home, or on business trips (e.g., '140' days) [video:06:10].
9.  **Commuting Days**: The system will calculate the 'Aufgesucht an Tagen' (Days visited) (e.g., '111' days) [video:06:15].
10. **Simple Distance**: Enter the one-way distance in kilometers (e.g., '17' km) [video:06:15].
11. **Distance by Public Transport/Other**: If applicable, enter the distance covered by public transport, motorcycle, bicycle, or as a pedestrian/carpool passenger (e.g., '17' km) [video:06:20].
12. Confirm the entry.

## Special Expenses (Anlage Sonderausgaben) - Church Tax and Donations

The `Anlage Sonderausgaben` is for special expenses like church tax and donations [video:08:08]:

1.  Navigate to 'Anlage Sonderausgaben'.
2.  Go to '1 - Kirchensteuer' (Church Tax).
3.  Select 'Kirchensteuer laut Lohnsteuerbescheinigung steuerpflichtige Person / Ehemann / Person A' and re-enter the amount of church tax from your `Lohnsteuerbescheinigung` (e.g., '558,84 €') [video:08:15]. This ensures it's deducted.
4.  If you received a church tax refund in the previous year, you must also enter that amount here, as it reduces the deductible amount [video:08:30].
5.  Go to '2 - Zuwendungen (Spenden und Mitgliedsbeiträge)' (Donations and Membership Fees).
6.  Enter details of your donations, including the name of the charitable organization (e.g., 'Gemeinnützige Organisation') and the donated amount (e.g., '150 €'), based on your donation receipt [video:08:45]. Confirm the entry.

## Insurance Contributions (Anlage Vorsorgeaufwand) - Health, Nursing, Unemployment, Liability

The `Anlage Vorsorgeaufwand` captures contributions to various insurance schemes and retirement provisions [video:08:56]:

1.  Navigate to 'Anlage Vorsorgeaufwand'.
2.  Go to '1 - Beiträge zur Altersvorsorge' (Contributions to Retirement Provision).
    *   Select the taxpayer (e.g., 'Gordon Gekko').
    *   In 'Zeile 4', enter the `Arbeitnehmerbeitrag` (employee contribution) to statutory pension insurance (e.g., '4.185 €') [video:09:10]. This is found in `Nr. 23 a/b` of your `Lohnsteuerbescheinigung`.
    *   In 'Zeile 9', enter the `Arbeitgeberanteil` (employer contribution) to statutory pension insurance (e.g., '4.185 €') [video:09:15].
    *   Click 'Eintrag übernehmen'.
3.  Go to '2 - Beiträge zur inländischen gesetzlichen Kranken- und Pflegeversicherung' (Contributions to Domestic Statutory Health and Nursing Insurance).
    *   Select the taxpayer.
    *   In 'Zeile 11', enter `Arbeitnehmerbeiträge zu Krankenversicherungen` (employee contributions to health insurance) (e.g., '3.668 €') [video:09:30].
    *   In 'Zeile 13', enter `Arbeitnehmerbeiträge zu sozialen Pflegeversicherungen` (employee contributions to social nursing insurance) (e.g., '1.035 €') [video:09:35].
    *   Click 'Eintrag übernehmen'.
4.  Go to '3 - Beiträge zur inländischen privaten Kranken- und Pflegeversicherung' (Contributions to Domestic Private Health and Nursing Insurance) (if applicable).
    *   Select the taxpayer.
    *   In 'Zeile 27', enter contributions to optional services or supplementary insurance (e.g., private nursing insurance: '157 €') [video:09:50].
    *   Click 'Eintrag übernehmen'.
5.  Go to '7 - Weitere sonstige Vorsorgeaufwendungen' (Other Miscellaneous Provident Expenses).
    *   In 'Zeile 43', enter `Arbeitnehmerbeiträge zur Arbeitslosenversicherung` (employee contributions to unemployment insurance) (e.g., '585 €') [video:10:05].
    *   Scroll down to 'Beiträge (abzüglich steuerfreier Zuschüsse und erstatteter Beiträge) zu Unfall- und Haftpflichtversicherungen' (Contributions to accident and liability insurance).
    *   Enter details for any liability insurance (e.g., 'Haftpflichtversicherung' and '76 €') [video:10:15]. Confirm the entry.
