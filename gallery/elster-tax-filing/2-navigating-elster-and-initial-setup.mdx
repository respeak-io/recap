## Accessing ELSTER and Starting a New Declaration

1.  Log in to the ELSTER portal.
2.  Navigate to 'Meine Formulare' (My Forms) [video:02:20].
3.  Click on 'Neue Formulare starten' (Start New Forms) [video:02:20].
4.  Select 'Einkommensteuererklärung unbeschränkte Steuerpflicht (ESt 1 A)' (Income Tax Declaration Unlimited Tax Liability) under 'Einkommensteuer' [video:02:30].
5.  Choose the tax year (e.g., '2023') [video:02:40].
6.  If not importing data from a previous declaration, select 'Ohne Datenübernahme fortfahren' (Continue without data transfer) [video:02:45].
7.  Enter the name of the taxpayer (e.g., 'Gordon Gekko') [video:02:50].
8.  Click 'Ohne Anlagenassistent fortfahren' (Continue without annex assistant) and confirm to 'Anlagen selbst wählen' (Choose annexes manually) [video:02:55].

## Selecting Required Forms (Anlagen)

The tax declaration comprises a main form (`Hauptvordruck`) and several annexes (`Anlagen`) depending on your specific tax situation. For our fictional case, the following forms are selected [video:03:00]:

*   **Hauptvordruck**: The main form for general information.
*   **Anlage Vorsorgeaufwand**: For social security contributions and other insurance costs.
*   **Anlage Haushaltsnahe Beschäftigungsverhältnisse, Dienstleistungen und Handwerkerleistungen**: For household-related employment, services, and craft services (including rental utility costs).
*   **Anlage Sonderausgaben**: For special expenses like church tax and donations.
*   **Anlage N**: For employee income, wages, and income-related expenses (`Werbungskosten`).

After selecting these forms, scroll down and click 'Weiter' (Next), then 'Weiter ohne Bescheinigungen' (Continue without certificates) [video:03:15].

## Filling out the Main Form (Hauptvordruck) - Personal Details

The `Hauptvordruck` requires your personal and identifying information [video:03:20]:

1.  **Steuernummer (Tax Number)**: Enter your existing tax number. If you don't have one, select 'Neue Steuernummer beantragen' (Apply for a new tax number) and choose your state and tax office (e.g., 'Berlin', 'Friedrichshain-Kreuzberg') [video:03:20].
2.  **SteuerID (Tax ID)**: On 'Seite 1 - Steuerpflichtige Person', enter your 11-digit tax identification number and your date of birth (e.g., '21.04.1989') [video:03:30].
3.  **Name and Address**: Re-enter your full name and current address (e.g., 'Gordon Gekko', 'Prinzenstraße 103, 10969 Berlin') [video:03:40]. The address is important for calculating commuting expenses.
4.  **Profession**: Enter your occupation (e.g., 'Redakteur' - Editor) [video:03:50].
5.  **Religion**: Select your religious affiliation (e.g., 'Evangelisch' - Protestant) for church tax purposes [video:04:00].

## Filling out the Main Form (Hauptvordruck) - Bank Details (Optional)

On 'Seite 4 - Bankverbindung', you can provide your bank details. This is necessary if you expect a tax refund from the tax office. If you do not wish to provide this or expect no refund, you can check the box 'Es ist keine Bankverbindung vorhanden' (No bank details available) [video:04:10].

Once completed, you are done with the `Hauptvordruck` and can proceed to other annexes.
