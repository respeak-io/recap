## Entering Household-Related Expenses (Anlage Haushaltsnahe Aufwendungen)

This annex is used to declare costs for household services, craft services, and certain utility bill items. These can include cleaning services, garden maintenance, or components of your rental `Nebenkostenabrechnung`. [video:04:21]

1.  Navigate to 'Anlage Haushaltsnahe Aufwendungen'.
2.  Go to '2 - Haushaltsnahe Beschäftigungsverhältnisse / Dienstleistungen'.
3.  In 'Zeile 5', enter the specific service and its cost from your `Nebenkostenabrechnung` [video:04:30]. Examples from the case study:
    *   `Straßenreinigung` (Street cleaning): 44 €
    *   `Hauswart` (Caretaker): 305 €
    *   `Gartenpflege` (Garden maintenance): 19 €
    *   `Schneeräumung` (Snow removal): 7 €
4.  After each entry, click the checkmark or 'Weitere Daten hinzufügen' (Add more data) to add subsequent items [video:04:45].

Once all relevant expenses are entered, you are finished with this annex.

## Claiming Work Materials (Anlage N)

Under 'Anlage N', you can claim expenses for work materials (`Arbeitsmittel`) [video:06:28]:

1.  Navigate to 'Anlage N'.
2.  Go to '10 - Aufwendungen für Arbeitsmittel'.
3.  For items like a laptop, enter the type of material and the deductible amount. If an item is used partly for professional and partly for private purposes, only the professional percentage is deductible. For a laptop costing 800 € and 50% professionally used, enter 'Laptop' and '400 €' [video:06:40].

**Note**: For general work materials, a lump sum of **110 €** can often be entered without specific receipts, as this amount is typically accepted without further proof [video:07:00]. To do this, simply enter 'Arbeitsmittel ohne Belege' and '110 €'.

## Applying for Home Office Flat Rate (Anlage N)

If you worked from home, you can claim the Home Office flat rate (Tagespauschale bei beruflicher Tätigkeit im Homeoffice) [video:07:20]:

1.  Navigate to 'Anlage N'.
2.  Go to '12 - Tagespauschale (bei beruflicher Tätigkeit im Homeoffice)'.
3.  In 'Zeile 61', enter the number of days you worked exclusively from your home office. For the fictional case, this is '110' days [video:07:30].

## Other Work-Related Expenses (Anlage N)

Additional work-related expenses can be claimed under 'Weitere Werbungskosten' [video:07:44]:

1.  Navigate to 'Anlage N'.
2.  Go to '14 - Weitere Werbungskosten'.
3.  **Bank Account Management Fees (`Kontoführungsgebühr`)**: A flat rate of **16 €** can often be claimed without proof for bank account management fees, as this is commonly accepted [video:07:50]. Enter 'Kontoführungsgebühr' and '16 €'.
4.  **Mobile Phone Bill (`Handyrechnung`)**: If you use your personal mobile phone for work, a portion of the bill can be deducted. The example uses a 20% professional usage for a total of '50 €' [video:08:00]. Enter 'Handyrechnung' and '50 €'.

Confirm each entry with the checkmark.
