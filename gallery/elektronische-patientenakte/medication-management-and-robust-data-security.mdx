## Enhanced Medication Safety and Management

The ePA significantly enhances medication safety. Upon activation, an integrated medication list provides a digital overview of all medications prescribed and dispensed via e-prescriptions from that point onward. [video:01:45] This digital foundation is crucial for effectively identifying potential interactions between different medications, thereby preventing adverse drug events. [video:01:55]

In a subsequent update, the ePA for all will introduce a comprehensive medication plan. This plan will provide patients with precise details, such as which medications to take, at what times, and in what dosages. [video:02:15] (e.g., 'Take one tablet two times a day,' visually represented as 2x, 3x, 2x). [video:02:25]

## Data Security and Encryption Standards

Data within the ePA is subject to the highest security standards. All information is transmitted exclusively in an encrypted format, rigorously protecting it from unauthorized access. [video:02:35] This robust encryption ensures data integrity and patient privacy throughout its lifecycle.
